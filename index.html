Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a crypto exchange?

A white-label exchange can be launched in 4— 6 weeks. A fully custom CEX typically takes 3— 5 months depending on features. DEX and hybrid platforms vary based on smart contract complexity. We provide a detailed timeline after the discovery phase.

Do you provide post-launch support and maintenance?

Yes. We offer flexible maintenance packages including 24/7 monitoring, bug fixes, security patches, feature updates, and dedicated support channels. Most clients stay with us long-term as their technology partner.

What blockchain networks do you develop on?

We build on all major networks: Ethereum, Solana, BNB Chain, Polygon, Avalanche, Arbitrum, Optimism, zkSync, and more. We also develop custom private blockchains using Hyperledger Fabric and Quorum for enterprise clients.

How do you handle security and compliance?

Security is built into every layer — from smart contract audits and penetration testing to KYC/AML integration and regulatory compliance. We support GDPR, MiCA, FinCEN, and 30+ other regulatory frameworks across global jurisdictions.

Do you sign NDAs before project discussions?

Absolutely. We sign NDAs before any project discussion. Your ideas, business plans, and technical requirements are treated with complete confidentiality. We also offer fixed-price contracts so there are no surprises.

What is your pricing model?

We offer both fixed-price and time & materials contracts depending on project scope. After a free discovery call, we provide a detailed proposal with clear milestones and payment schedules. No hidden fees, ever.
